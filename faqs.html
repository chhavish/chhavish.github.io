Frequently Answered Questions

  • Why do I not see the changes made to my listing?

    Updates made to the listing are visible after refreshing. Close and open the app and you should see your changes.

  • How can I save my searches?

    Saving searches is a feature to be released in the upcoming version. Please switch on the auto-updates to get the latest releases.

  • Why do I have to enter my phone number to chat?

    The app uses a third party char framework which registers users using their phone numbers. Phone number is required to register and validate a user before the chat feature can be used.

  • Will I be charged if I rent a place from the app?

    For renters there is no fee.

  • Will I be charged for listing a place in the app?

    There are no charges for listing. The linked account or card will be charged whenever a listing is rented from the app.

  • How much is the per month price for subscription?

    The charges are calculated based on the number of listings.
